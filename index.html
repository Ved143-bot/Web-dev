What Is Farming

Farming

Farming is the practice of cultivating land, raising crops, and rearing animals for food, fiber, and other products. It involves soil preparation, planting, irrigation, pest control, and harvesting. Farming sustains human life, supports economies, and shapes landscapes, utilizing traditional or modern techniques to meet global food and resource demands.

Importance

Farming is essential for producing food, raw materials, and livelihoods. It ensures global food security, supports economies, and sustains rural communities. Farming preserves biodiversity, fosters environmental stewardship, and addresses hunger. As the population grows, sustainable farming practices are crucial to meet demand while protecting natural resources and combating climate change.

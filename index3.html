Seasonal Crops Guide

Different crops thrive in different seasons, requiring specific climate conditions and water levels. Here is a guide to major crops and their suitable seasons.

Crop Season Water Requirement Climate Condition Soil Type Soil pH
Wheat Winter (Rabi) 450-600 mm Cool and dry climate Loamy soil 6.0-7.5
Rice Monsoon (Kharif) 1200-2000 mm Hot and humid Clayey soil 5.0-6.5
Maize Summer & Rainy 500-800 mm Warm climate Sandy loam soil 5.5-7.0
Cotton Summer & Monsoon (Kharif) 700-1300 mm Warm and dry climate Black soil 6.0-7.5
Barley Winter (Rabi) 300-500 mm Cool and dry Well-drained loamy soil 6.0-8.0
Potatoes Winter (Rabi) 400-600 mm Cool climate Sandy loam soil 5.0-6.5
Sugarcane Summer & Monsoon 1500-2500 mm Hot and humid Loamy soil 6.5-7.
